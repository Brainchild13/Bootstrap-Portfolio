About Me

My name is David and I have a love affair with technology. I've been building websites for 15 years. Wordpress has been my weapon of choice but recently I decided to expand my arsenal by enrolling in the UCLA Coding Bootcamp to become a Full-Stack Developer, utilizing the MERN Stack (Mongo DB, Express.js React.js, and Node.js). I plan to conquer my piece of the world with these new tools.


My goal is to become part of a productive team whose goal is to make life better for humanity through technology. I particulary look forward to the opportunity to learn.
